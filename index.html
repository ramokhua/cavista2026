Core Features

Three Pillars of Protection

An AI-powered system that catches what clinics miss

Pillar 01
Generational Risk Engine
Input your family's health tree — parents, grandparents, siblings — and our logistic regression model generates personalised risk percentages for Type 2 Diabetes, Hypertension, Cardiovascular Disease, and Chronic Kidney Disease. Your ancestors become your health compass.
📊
Pillar 02
Remote Monitoring
Log vitals, track symptoms, record illness history. AI detects dangerous thresholds and emerging patterns before they become emergencies.

AI Health Companion
Chat with a context-aware AI that knows your history, explains risks in plain language, and guides preventive action.
